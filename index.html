Biography

I am currently an Associate Professor with the Department of Computer Science and Technology, Nanjing Tech University, Nanjing, China. Before that, I received my Ph.D. degree from Jiangnan University, Wuxi, China, in 2017. I has been a research assistant in the School of Nurseing (Center of smart health), Hong Kong Polytechnic University. My current research interests include Artificial intelligence, deep learning, transfer learning, medical image analysis and biomedical signal processing.

Now, I am dedicated to designing data-efficient learning methods for biomedical image analysis and EEG signal processing. If you would like to work with me, feel free to send me an email.
